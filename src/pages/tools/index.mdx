A variety of web-based tools I've made and found useful.
Mostly vibecoded.

Inspired by [Simon Willison](https://tools.simonwillison.net/).

1. [Cube Cobbler](/tools/cubecobbler) - for checking Magic cards off a list and finding out which ones you still need.
2. [Surge](/tools/surge) - Surge XT synthesizer, ported to WebAssembly ([see repo](https://github.com/ectucker1/surge/tree/clap-wasm-sidemodule)).
3. [Transpose](/tools/transpose) - tranposes musical notes written in plaintext (e.g. `A, A#, Bb`).
